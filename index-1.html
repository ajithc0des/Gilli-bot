Terms & Conditions

1. Service Overview

OSINT Premium provides access to OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) tools for informational and educational purposes only. Premium keys grant access for the purchased duration.

2. Payment Policy

All payments are processed via UPI. Once a UTR (transaction ID) is submitted, admin manually verifies the payment. Approval typically takes within a few hours.

  • Pay the exact amount shown — no more, no less.
  • Submit your correct UTR after payment.
  • Duplicate or fraudulent UTR submissions will result in a ban.

3. Refund Policy

All sales are final. No refunds once a Premium Key is delivered. If payment is rejected, your money is not deducted — UPI rejections are instant.

4. Key Usage
